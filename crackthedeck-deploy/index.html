Basic plan (AI scoring + PDF) is delivered instantly. Pro and Expert reviews include a human investor review and are delivered within 24 hours via email.

Who reviews my deck?

Your deck is reviewed by professionals with direct investment experience — people who have evaluated hundreds of startups, served on boards, and participated in investment committee decisions across 10+ countries.
